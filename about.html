Our Story

Sugar & Victoria began with one simple idea: build a welcoming independent cafe where every sweet bite and every cup of coffee feels thoughtfully made. In the heart of Stourport-on-Severn, we have become known for warm service, beautiful desserts, and a cosy space that feels like a local secret worth sharing.

Independent and Heartfelt

As a proudly independent business, we care deeply about quality, consistency, and atmosphere. Guests visit for cakes and coffee, but return for the familiar smiles, relaxed vibe, and that comforting cafe feel.

Whether you are dropping in for a quick pastry, a slow afternoon dessert, or a light lunch, the team is always ready to make you feel at home.

A Place to Pause

From weekday coffee breaks to weekend catch-ups, Sugar & Victoria is designed for sweet moments. Soft details, boutique styling, and homemade flavour all come together to create an experience that is both modern and comforting.
