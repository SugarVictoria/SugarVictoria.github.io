Cookie Policy

Last updated: 13 March 2026

How We Use Cookies and Similar Storage

We use essential browser storage to remember your cookie preference. Optional functional cookies/storage are only used if you choose to allow them.

Cookie Categories

Essential: Required for core site operation, including storing your consent choice.
Functional (optional): Used to load third-party embeds such as Google Maps.

Services That May Be Enabled by Functional Consent

Google Maps embed on our Visit page. When enabled, Google may receive technical data such as IP address and device metadata.

How to Change Your Choice

Use the control shown in the website footer. You can also clear site data in your browser settings.

Storage Key We Use

The site stores your preference in local storage under: sv_cookie_consent_v1.
